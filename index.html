YOU ARE

You are a real go-getter and you love a challenge. You are passionate and you love to show people a good time by taking them outside and helping them push their limits.


You lack exposure and struggle to sell your adventure online. You are looking for a way to showcase your adventure and sell your time online, realizing your dream.

WHAT WE DO

As an adventure provider it can be very hard. There are so many ways to sell your adventures, but where do you start? Which path do you take? When should you start and how should you start? We work closely with you to understand what you are trying to sell so we can develop and create a successful adventure listing. With our help, you can be selling on weAdventure in only a few days.

HOW WE DO IT

Our goal is to help you give you all the tools you need to sell adventure listings on the weAdventure marketplace. Together with you we help you create a short action packed GoPro video to market your company and create and collect all of the necessary information and media to make an awesome adventure listing.


Once your listing is live, we will make sure your adventure gets the exposure it needs. With our guidance, you will learn how to use weAdventure to take bookings directly from travelers.

WHY WE DO IT

We are pioneers... adventurers... and entrepreneurs, just like you. We know of your struggles, we’ve been down this road before.


Not only is your adventure great for you, it’s also great for travelers, as it gives people a chance to contribute directly to the local community and also to a project they believe in. They get to take an active role in helping bring your passion to life. We love to connect through weAdventure, and we're sure you will love it too!

WHAT IT COSTS

Your sweat, dedication and hard work... that's all we need from you. We are co-financed by different government bodies and we believe that with the right storytelling and service, you will be successful.

On our marketplace, we charge adventure travelers a fixed 10% booking fee, on top of your adventure price. This booking fee is 100% re-invested into weAdventure and making it better for the future.
